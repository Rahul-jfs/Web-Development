Borders

Border allows us to change the Width, color, border-style.

p{border: 2px solid red;}

We can also make the corners round by using border-radius.

Margin

Margins are used to create space around elements, outside of any defined borders.

margin: 10px 15px; Here the margin 10px will be applied to the top and bottom, while 15px will be applied to left and right

margin: 10px; Here the margin 10px will be applied to all the sides of the element

margin: 10px 12px 15px; Here the margin 10px will be applied to the top, while 12px will be applied to left and right and 15px will be applied to bottom

Padding

Padding is used to create space around an element's content, inside of any defined borders.

Same syntax of margin applies to the padding
